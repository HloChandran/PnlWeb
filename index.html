✦ Features

Everything a Serious Trader Needs

From quick trade logging to deep performance analytics — PnL. has every tool to help you improve.

📊

Smart P&L Tracking

Log BUY and SELL trades with real-time P&L calculation. See your portfolio value update live with a beautiful animated counter on the Home screen.

🧠

Deep Analytics

Win rate, profit factor, Sharpe ratio, max drawdown, expectancy, cumulative P&L curve — over 40 metrics computed across 1W, 1M, 1Y, or All time.

🛡️

Discipline Mode

Set daily trade limits, per-trade amount caps, block revenge trading, and auto-stop after consecutive losses. Build the habits that protect your capital.

🔒

App Lock

Protect your sensitive trade data with a 4-digit PIN or biometric (fingerprint/face) lock. The app locks automatically when you put it in the background.

🌍

Multi-Currency

Supports 150+ global currencies. Switch your display currency from the Profile screen — all trade values update instantly across the entire app.

📅

Trade History & Filters

Browse all your trades filtered by Day, Week, or Month. Filter by Winning or Losing trades. View bar charts of hourly or daily P&L at a glance.

💾

Cloud Backup

Authenticated users can back up all trade data to the cloud via Firebase and restore it on any device. Your journal is always safe.

📝

Trading Rules Notepad

Add personal trading rules with icons and colours. Keep your rules visible on the Home screen so you stay grounded before every trade.

🌙

Light & Dark Themes

Full Material 3 light and dark mode support. Your preference is saved and applied instantly every time you open the app.

✦ Discipline Mode

Protect Your Capital From Yourself

Discipline Mode turns your trading rules into enforced guardrails — so you stop overtrading before it costs you.

🎯

Daily Trade Limit

Set a maximum number of trades per day. When you hit the limit, the Add Trade button locks automatically.

💰

Per-Trade Amount Cap

Define the maximum capital you can deploy on a single trade. Quick presets: ₹25k, ₹50k, ₹1L.

🚫

Block Revenge Trading

Toggle this on and the app will warn you when you're about to trade immediately after a loss — the most common self-destructive pattern.

🛑

Stop After N Losses

Automatically lock trading after two consecutive losses, giving you time to step back and reassess.

✦ Security & Privacy

Your Data. Your Control.

PnL. is designed with privacy and security at its core. Your trade data stays on your device by default.

🔐

Biometric & PIN Lock

Protect the app with fingerprint, face ID, or a 4-digit passcode. Locks instantly when you leave the app.

📱

On-Device Storage

All trade data is stored locally in SQLite on your device. No trade data leaves your phone without your explicit action.

☁️

Opt-in Cloud Backup

Cloud backup is entirely optional. Only signed-in users can enable it, and you can restore or delete your backup at any time.

🔑

Firebase Auth

Sign in with email/password or Google. Your account is protected by Firebase Authentication — we never store raw passwords.

🗑️

Delete Account

Want to leave? Delete your account and all associated cloud data permanently — directly from the app or via our deletion page.
