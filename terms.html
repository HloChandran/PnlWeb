2. About PnL.

PnL. is a personal trade journalling and analytics application designed for retail traders. It allows users to:

  • Log stock and derivatives trades manually.
  • Track daily Profit & Loss (P&L) summaries.
  • Review trade history with filters and charts.
  • Analyse performance metrics including win rate, profit factor, Sharpe ratio, and more.
  • Enforce personal trading discipline through configurable guardrails.
  • Back up and restore trade data via optional cloud sync.

PnL. is a journalling tool only. It is not a brokerage platform, trading platform, or financial advisory service. All trade data is entered manually by the user.
